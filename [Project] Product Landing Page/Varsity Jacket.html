About Varsity Jackets

Varsity Jackets, which were also known as Letterman Jackets, are a type of clothing originating from High-schools and Universities. It is mostly known to be worn by students that participated in 'Varsity' level sports, Academics, or other activities that qualify the participant to earn an official Varsity Jacket.

Many Universities and High Schools, mostly have Varsity teams that compete in competitions. Within those, are the best players or individuals that represent their teams and thus, Varsity Jackets helps classify this group of people within our view.
